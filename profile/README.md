### What is Unalome?

AI agents are writing code, reading emails, managing files, and making decisions on our behalf — at superhuman speed. Most people can't answer basic questions: *What did my agent just do? What data did it access? How much did it cost?*

**Unalome builds free, open-source tools that give regular people clarity and control over their AI agents.** No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Everything runs locally on your machine.

### Products

**Agent Firewall** — *See what your agents see.*
Monitor every action, track costs, scan for threats, detect PII leaks, and kill rogue agents — all from one dashboard. Supports Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and more.

**Agent Bench** — *Test before you trust.* (Coming soon)
Benchmark and evaluate AI agents side by side. Compare speed, accuracy, cost, and safety across models and frameworks.

### Our Values

- **Privacy first** — Your data stays on your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no data collection.
- **Free forever** — No paywalls, no premium tiers. Every tool is Apache 2.0 licensed.
- **For everyone** — AI tools shouldn't just be for developers. We build for regular people too.
- **Community driven** — Built in the open. PRs welcome for new agent parsers and features.
